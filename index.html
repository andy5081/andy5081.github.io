ABOUT

King’s Bakery was established in 2015 with a vision to serve high quality baked goods at an extremely affordable price. What sets King’s Bakery apart from other bakeries is our ability to produce FRESH baked goods every day. Every product is made fresh every morning by our renowned pastry chefs before we put it on our shelves for sale. We believe customers should be able to enjoy high quality pastries at reasonable prices.
